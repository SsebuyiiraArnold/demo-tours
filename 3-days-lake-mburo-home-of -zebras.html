Package Detail

3 Days Lake Mburo home of Zebras

  • 3 days / 2 night
  • people: 20
  • Uganda
DESCRIPTION
PROGRAM
TRIP INCLUDES
DESCRIPTION

This 3 days safari will take you to Lake Mburo National Park which is located in Mbarara and Kiluhura Districts in western Uganda. This location is approximately 240 kilometres (150 mi), by road, west of Kampala,which is a one of Uganda’s smallest parks located in the western Uganda, though it’s the best known as the home to zebra, eland and impala, as well as an abundance of other animals and various bird species

PROGRAM

Day 1: Drive to Lake Mburo
Your driver will pick you up from the airport, lodge, and hotel at your place of stay within Kampala. And Entebbe, will give you a briefing about the journey and the trip then you will drive to Lake Mburo National Park, which is a one of Uganda’s smallest parks located in the western Uganda, though it’s the best known as the home to zebra, eland and impala, as well as an abundance of other animals and various bird species.
Driving time: 4 to 5 hours

Accommodation
Mid-range: – Rwakobo Rock
Meal Plan: – Dinner

Day 2: Game Drive & Boat Trip
Early morning after your breakfast, you will go for a game drive with a ranger helping you to spot out some different animal species commonly zebra, impala, buffalo, elands, and other ungulates also roan antelope and leopard, hyenas and jackals. Bird viewing opportunities will also be available for birders.

In the afternoon, after your fantastic lunch from your lodge, you will take a boat trip as a leisure for you relaxing, view some animals like crocodiles, hippos etc, in the evening you will have you dinner and over night.

Accommodation
Mid-range: – Rwakobo Rock
Meal Plan: – Breakfast & Dinner

Day 3: Drive Back To Kampala
In the Early morning you will go for a nature walking safari the common ones to be seen, are zebra, impala, buffalo, topi, and elands as herbivores not to leave out the rare slightly of roan antelope and leopard, hyenas and jackals. Bird viewing opportunities are excellent for birders.

After Late breakfast at your lodge you will start your journey back to Kampala. On your way, you will have lunch, and then continue. You will get an opportunity to stop at the Equator; you take photo shoots and buying souvenirs. You willthenbe dropped to the airport for your flight back home
