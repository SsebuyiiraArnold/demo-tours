About us

WHO WE ARE

Trion Safaris Limited

*/

Trion Safaris Uganda is a specialist luxury tour operator that handcrafts the most authentic African holiday itineraries and exclusive travel experiences throughout the entire continent. With over 10 years of know-how and the best connections in the industry, we have a passion for Africa and are always up to date on its latest offerings. Africa is a collection of some of the most amazing countries and cultures on earth. At Trion Safaris Uganda, we believe traveling does not necessarily mean going from one place to another, rather, experiencing new things that you could miss; wildlife, local communities, their culture and the surrounding nature/land. Your precious time spent with us will leave you with great memories and a different person after enjoying a great adventure on African land. We know Africa, we love Africa, and we want to share it with you.

ULTIMATE GUIDE TO EPIC ADVENTURE

Why Travel With Us

PERSONAL SERVICE

We provide personalized and bespoke travel experiences, offering custom itineraries and the luxury of experiencing business class throughout your journey.

SUSTAINABILITY

Committed to responsible tourism, we actively contribute to the conservation of Uganda’s natural resources and wildlife, ensuring that our travel practices align with principles of environmental preservation.

AFFORDABLE PRICE

Our Best Price Guarantee ensures you secure the most competitive rates, coupled with transparent pricing and a commitment to delivering exceptional value for your dream vacation.

BEST DESTINATION

Navigating a world of fascinating destinations, we specialize in curating itineraries that showcase stunning scenery, cultural richness, and unique experiences, guiding you to the perfect vacation spot tailored to your preferences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that each of our client gets a unique and unforgettable safari experience in Uganda by providing the most affordable and authentic tours based on the local knowledge

Promoting African Talent

Trion Safari Uganda, a sustainable-based company, not only focuses on wildlife, culture, nature and primates, but there is so much more to discover in Uganda, the African communities are full of talent! We try to develop their talents by giving them chances to exhibit their Art pieces. These can act as your souvenirs when you return to your home country. We recommend you pay a visit to their Artwork Shop and have a look at their exhibitions in their Art Gallery if you are interested.

Our Tour guides/drivers

As also a Destination Management Company (DMC), we tend to use local Tour guides who are well-trained from vital tourism institutes to ensure that they are well equipped with knowledge of the various Tourism products and destinations. They are all patient, strong, intelligent, knowledgeable and well conversant with different tourist destinations and all the safari logistics.

Our guides are all knowledgeable with different language skills like; English, Spanish, German, Italian or French, we speak your language. We have a guide to whom you can communicate your every need.

From their hard work and passion about the travel industry has helped us grow to become one of the fast growing young and most trusted tour operator and partner in East Africa and we look forward to serving you.

 

Our Safari Vehicles

All safari vehicles we use are fully comprehensive insured, 4x4WD well raised from the ground making suitable for the African bumpy roads. We use Safari jeeps and Min-Vans and range from 1 to 7 seating capacity. They are all customized, with Pop up a roof which gives a wide angle for game and site viewing. And we always maintain them in perfect mechanical condition to enable our clients have their comfortable African holidays.
