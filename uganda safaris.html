Uganda Safaris

UGANDA - THE PEARL OF AFRICA

Visitors often fall in love with Uganda’s breathtaking Rift Valley scenery, lush countryside, and incredible biodiversity, making up dozens Places to Visit in Uganda. This enchanting destination has recently gained recognition from several media and professionals as a must-visit travel destination, thanks to its friendly people, the endangered Mountain Gorillas and chimpanzees, and other attractions, including the Uganda safaris tour packages. You can also spot the big five, big cats, and an incredible variety of over 1,066 species of birds. Don’t miss the opportunity to experience the fabulous River Nile, immerse in the country’s rich cultures, and explore many more enticing sights.
